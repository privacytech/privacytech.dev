{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead color="primary" %}} Data being one of the most valuable assets, is often subject to misuse. Many countries have passed their data privacy laws to protect the data rights of their citizens. [EU GDPR](https://gdpr-info.eu/), [California CCPA](https://www.oag.ca.gov/privacy/ccpa),[Brazil LGPD](https://iapp.org/news/a/an-overview-of-brazils-lgpd/). Businesses around the world are trying to implement the privacy APIs and controlls. To our big surprise - there is no strong open source community around the privacy technologies.

Our goal: Build open source community to the rescue!
